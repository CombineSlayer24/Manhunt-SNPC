Music file, used to have an authentic feel like if you were actually playing Manhunt.

Use this addon.
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=104603291

In spawnmenu go to "Spawnlists" tab, go to "Browse Sounds", in "Legacy Addons", click on vj_manhunt_snpc

then click on music > stems and there you go.

I only provided two level OST (Born Again and Doorway to Hell) for this demo/preview.
